# Cost constraint

The user pays $0 out of pocket for this project. Only use services/libraries within their free tier or free limits — Clerk, Neon Postgres, and Vercel hosting/blob are fine as long as usage stays under their free-tier caps. Do not add new paid dependencies, or features/usage patterns that would push existing services (auth MAUs, DB storage, hosting/bandwidth) past their free limits, without flagging it first.
